WHO reports a rise in Ebola cases in West Africa

In English中文
The number of reported Ebola cases has increased for the first time this year.据报道，埃博拉病例数目今年首次增加。
The world health organization says 124 new cases were reported in the week ending February 1.世界卫生组织说，截止2月1号报告了124份新病例。
There were 80 new cases in Sierra Leone, 39 in Guinea and five in Liberia.在塞拉利昂有80例，印古尼亚39例，利比里亚5例。
On Wednesday the WHO said there is an urgent need to end the outbreak before the rainy season begins.周三世界卫生组织表示迫切需要在雨季开始前结束疫情。
Rural areas are harder to reach during that period.在雨季，乡村地区更难到达。
Since the beginning of the Ebola outbreak， there have been almost 22,500 reported cases in the three countries, and nearly 9,000 death。自埃博拉疫情爆发以来，这三个国家有报告了近22，500项病例，近9，000人死亡。
The number of new cases had gone down for several weeks，raising hopes the outbreak was ending.新病例的数量已经下降了几周，提升了疫情结束的希望。
